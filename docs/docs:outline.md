ntroduction to Time Management
Course Duration: 1 hour
Target Audience: University students
Objective: Equip students with essential time management skills to improve productivity, reduce stress, and achieve academic success.

Session 1: Why Time Management Matters (20 min)
The consequences of poor time management on academic performance and mental health
Common time management challenges faced by university students
Benefits of effective time management: increased productivity, better work-life balance, and goal achievement
Real-life examples of successful time management in student life
Session 2: The Main Techniques (20 min)
Prioritization methods: Eisenhower Matrix and ABC analysis
Time blocking and scheduling techniques
The Pomodoro Technique for focused work sessions
Avoiding procrastination: strategies like breaking tasks into smaller steps and setting deadlines
Session 3: Creating Your Plan (20 min)
Assessing your current time usage with a time audit
Setting SMART goals for academic and personal life
Creating a personalized weekly schedule and to-do list
Tools and apps for time management: digital calendars, task managers, and productivity apps
Review and adjustment: tracking progress and making necessary changes to your plan
